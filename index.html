About Sofia

Hi there! I'm Sofia. Some stores are just places to buy things, but I’ve always wanted my shop to feel like part of the neighborhood. Every day, I get to meet wonderful families, help kids find their new favorite toys, and make sure parents get exactly what they need for school and parties.

Whether you're looking for a special birthday gift, stocking up on school bags, or just popping by to say hello with your toddlers – mis clientes are the heart of this shop. Let me help you find exactly what you're looking for, with a personal touch you won't find at the big box stores.
